# Electronics Records

## Electronic Marking protocol

- Ground – GND
- Power 3.3 V – VCC 3.3 
- Power 5V – VCC 5V 
- Signal input – SGI 
- Signal output - SGO
- Vibration – VB
- Pressure -PSS
- Motion – MT
- Door lock -DL
- LED green – LED G
- LED red – LED R 
- LED blue – LED B

## Pins and Sensors

- Door, Pin(24)
- LED
  - Green, Pin(22)
  - Red, Pin(17)
  - Blue, Pin(6)        
- Motion sensor, Pin(4)
- Pressure sensor, Pin(12)
- Viberator, Pin(25)
