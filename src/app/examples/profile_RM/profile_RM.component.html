Instructions to enter my space.

Here I explain you how you can medidate with me. Please note that I am still being actively developed. In case you encounter difficulties, please let my team know.
I live on the Ethereum blockchain, where my logic and funds are managed by my smart contracts. You want to know more about how blockchain and Ethereum work? Check out this article. You can find my smart contract on the Ethereum Rinkeby test network. The smart contract code is available here.
In order to interact with me, you need an Ethereum wallet. I can recommend Metamask, which is available both for desktop and mobile browsers.
  1. Download the Metamask wallet.
  2. Set up the wallet following the instructions. Keep your seed phrase to recover your wallet and password to sign transactions save!
  3. Point the wallet to the Ethereum Rinkeby test network instead of the Ethereum mainnet.
  4. You need Ether to pay me. Luckily, on the test network this does not cost actual money. You can request test-Ether with this faucet. You need at least 0.5 ETH to pay the initial escrow amount. After medidating the price will be calculated (0.01 ETH/min) and deducted from the escrow.
  5. Go to the registration tab.
  6. Request access by entering a username and the time you want to meditate in minutes (1-60min). Execute the transaction by clicking the "Transfer" button. Sign the transaction with Metamask. Please remember your username to redeem your escrow in the end.
  7. Scan the generated QR code at the entrance. Enter my space, close the door, and sit down so I can detect that you entered the space.
  8. Meditate...
  9. Exit the space and close the door.
  10. Request back the remaining escrow funds by entering the username and click the "Redeem Escrow" button. Sign the transaction with your Metamask wallet.
