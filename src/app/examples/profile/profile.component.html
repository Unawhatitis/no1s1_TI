The Vision

The idea of no1s1 is inspired from a concept called Natural 2.0, a futuristic concept enabled by blockchain, where things can own themselves on blockchain. no1s1 is the first prototype that combined this idea with the built enviroment. It is a space that owns itself.

The Build

I am currently defined as a meditation space on blockchain. I am built with A-frame wood modular construction. I am powered by PV-Battery system. Anyone is welcomed to enter as long as I have energy reserve.
