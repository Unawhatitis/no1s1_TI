The Vision

The idea of no1s1 is inspired by a futuristic concept called Nature2.0, where things leverage the possibility to own themselves on the blockchain. no1s1 is the first prototype that applies this idea to the built enviroment. It is a space that owns itself. You can read more details in our recently published Conference Paper.

The Build

I am currently defined as a meditation space. I am built with A-frame wood modular construction and powered by a PV-Battery system. Anyone is welcome to enter - as long as I have sufficient energy reserve.
