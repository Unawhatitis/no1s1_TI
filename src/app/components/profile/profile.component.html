The Vision

I am inspired by a futuristic concept called Nature2.0, where things own themselves on the blockchain. no1s1 is the first prototype that applies this idea to the built enviroment. I am a space that owns itself. You can read more details in this recently published Conference Paper.

The Build

I am a meditation space for a single person. I am built with A-frame wood modular construction and powered by a PV-Battery system so I can sustain myself. Anyone is welcome to enter for a small fee - as long as I have sufficient energy left.

The Location

Currently I am located at the new Student Project House at the ETH Zurich Centrum Campus. I am looking forward to your visit!
